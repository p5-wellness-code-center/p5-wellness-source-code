What is P5 wellness ?

A Platform committed to support Women in 5 important phases of her life cycle which are based on the reproductive cycle, beginning with menstruation and ending with menopause.

  • Puberty & Teen
  • Pre-pregnancy
  • Pregnancy
  • Post-Pregnancy
  • Pre & Post menopause

We at P5wellness understand your health concerns very well and committed to assist you with best available solutions.

We as a team of gynaecologists, wellness experts, Applied & Clinical nutritionists, Ayurveda & Naturopathy doctors, Life coach & Metaphysical counsellors, Physiotherapists support your Physical and Emotional wellbeing.


Vision


To provide a comprehensive healthcare ecosystem which enables women to take informed decisions independently about their own health across all life stages.


Mission


To create a candid & secure digital space where the women across all life stages finds it convenient to get assisted with best available health solutions, by bringing together verified and experienced range of health experts at one place.
